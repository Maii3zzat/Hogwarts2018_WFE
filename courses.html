Charms

Students learn spells from textbooks, wand movements and spells pronunciation. Taught by professor Flitwick.

Defense Against the Dark Arts

Students learn about dark arts, some dark creatures and some defensive spells. Taught by professor Lupin and others.

Herbology

The study of magical plants, how to raise them and important ingrediants for potion making. Taught by professor Sprout.

Transfiguration

The collection of magical spells used to change objects to other objects. Taught by professor McGonagall.

Potions

The study of potion making, in this class students learn to brew potions the correct way by mixing magical ingredients. Taught by professor Snape.

About

Hogwarts school was founded in the 9th/10th century, located in Scotland, Europe.
Students can go there using Hogwarts Express at Platform 9 ¾, King's Cross Station.
The school gives children with magical abilities sufficient training to enrich their magic.
The school accepts students from the age of 11 and the educating process continues until
the age of 18. Students get accepted into the school using an owl post sent to them at age 11.
Students get sorted into four different houses.
