About houses

Gryffindor  Slytherin  Ravenclaw  Hufflepuff

Are the four houses students get sorted into. Each year, houses compete for the house cup
by earning and losing points throughout the year in various events.
The biggest of which is Quidditch team, as each house has his own quidditch team
and competes for the Inter-House Quidditch Cup.

The founders of the four houses used to pick students themselves.
Worrying of the sorting process after their deaths, Godric Gryffindor took his hat off
and each of the four creators put some brains and thoughts into the hat, eventually creating
the Sorting Hat to sort students itself into houses.








Gryffindor

The house of Gryffindor was first founded by Godric Gryffindor.
The house is symbolized by the lion, which makes Gryffindor
the house of bravery, daring and nerve.

Slytherin

Salazar Slytherin was the one who founded Slytherin House.
Slytherin's symbol is the serpent. Students who get sorted into
Slytherin mainly have ambition,cunning and resourcefulness.

Ravenclaw

The house of Ravenclaw was first founded by Rowena Ravenclaw. The eagle is Ravenclaw's symbolic
animal. The house of Ravenclaw is famous by
its intelligent, wise, and wit students.

Hufflepuff

Helga Hufflepuff was the founder of Hufflepuff House. The emblematic animal
of Hufflepuff is the badger. Its characteristics are hard work, dedication, patience,
loyalty and finally fair play.

About

Hogwarts school was founded in the 9th/10th century, located in Scotland, Europe.
Students can go there using Hogwarts Express at Platform 9 ¾, King's Cross Station.
The school gives children with magical abilities sufficient training to enrich their magic.
The school accepts students from the age of 11 and the educating process continues until
the age of 18. Students get accepted into the school using an owl post sent to them at age 11.
Students get sorted into four different houses.
